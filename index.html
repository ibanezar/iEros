The Craft

Metal, light,
and shadow.

Every IEROS light is precision laser-cut from steel, then powder-coated for permanent outdoor durability. The geometric pattern is the product — it doesn't just emit light, it sculpts it.

The shadow cast on your wall is as much the design as the fixture itself. As the sun moves, the shadow shifts — a different artwork each hour of the day.
